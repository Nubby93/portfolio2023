The Pirelli Calendar is a prestigious and exclusive calendar that features photographs of high-end fashion models and celebrities.

My role

I am part of the team in charge of designing and developing the platform journalists use to download media assets (photos, videos, press releases). For the 2023 edition I designed a brand new UI, and assisted my collegue with the developing process.

Sensitive information

Due to the sensitive nature of the project, I am unable to present the whole process, tools, and materials. This project features only snippets of the initial prototype presented to the stackeholders and imagery that has already been released to the public.

Art direction

The art direction and photography of the Pirelli Calendar often evoke a mood of sophistication, elegance and creativity.

Design system

To aid in the design process I build on top of the 8px grid a small, but incredibly useful design system.

Embodying the Pirelli Spirit

To match the elegance and sophistication, I used imagery of flowers, a black & white scheme, and a chic typeface influenced by calligraphy and hand lettering that transforms text into art.

Designing for cultural diversity

The written materials are available in 9 languages, each with its own thumbnail. The gradient overlay makes the flag stand out, which makes scanning the page easy.

The design language

My approach was "minimum UI, maximum impact", turning the Pirelli Calendar into a simple, yet visually driven platform.
