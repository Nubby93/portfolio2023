Since it is a solo project, I am in charge of everything from designing a logo, to developing the website.

1-D: Define

Tone and voice

Neobrutalism is a design trend that gained momentum at the same time as I was developing the idea of UIBoat, so it seemed right to go in that direction. I chose a black and white scheme, with a splash of blue for colour, and a neo-grotesk typeface.

2-D: Design

Brand Identity

UIBoat's online identity revolves around the B&W scheme and neo-grotesk type. The splashes of colour come from eye-catching photography and bright visuals.

2-D: Design

High fidelity designs

I created a small design system made of styles for text and colour, components, and variants. After that, I turned the wireframes in high fidely designs by adding body copy, colour, imagery, and motion.
